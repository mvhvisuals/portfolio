How to Make Tonkotsu Ramen

Preparation

1. Cook the tonkotsu broth

In the Instant Pot, bring the broth ingredients up to pressure (takes 40 minutes), then cook the tonkotsu broth ingredients under high pressure for 1 hour

2. Cold brew dashi

Meanwhile, start the cold brew dashi (Japanese soup stock).

3. Make the shoyu tarei

Make the shoyu tare (soy sauce-flavored seasoning sauce)..

Cooking

4. After pressure cooking

After pressure cooking, uncover the broth and boil over high heat (Saut√© mode) for 30 minutes. Skim the scum and foam.

5. Add seasoning & dashi liquid

Add the hidden seasoning ingredients and the dashi solid ingredients. Bring it to a rolling boil. After 30 minutes, add the dashi liquid. Boil for 30 minutes more and stir to blend the broth components.

6. Strain the tonkotsu ramen broth.

Strain the tonkotsu ramen broth.

Assemble

4. Boil the ramen noodles

Boil the ramen noodles to your desired doneness. Add the shoyu tare in individual bowls..

5. Serve the noodles

Serve the noodles with hot broth.

6. Add your toppings

Add your toppings and serve immediately..
